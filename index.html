PG student positions available: I am looking for self-motivated Master's students with a strong theoretical background in physics, math, and machine learning, or with a strong systems background in electromagnetic field using electromagnetic simulation software (COMSOL, CST, et al.). Please send me your CV, representative publications (if any), detailed transcripts with GPA and ranking, GRE/TOEFL/CET scores, along with a brief introduction about yourself if you are interested in working with me. Undergraduate students at School of Information Science and Engineering are encouraged to discuss with me about potential research projects. I am also interested in hosting visiting students.

Our group uses experimental methods combined with theoretical calculations to study the THz characteristics, microwave absorption characteristics and electromagnetic manipulation of soft-functional optoelectronic materials ionic liquids.
